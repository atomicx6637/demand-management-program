# Enhancement Tier Quick Reference
## Small Investments, Fast Decisions (<$50k)

**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Audience:** Product Teams, Business Units
**Questions?** Contact your Product Team Lead or EA team

---

## What Qualifies as an Enhancement?

âœ… **You should submit an Enhancement request if your need is:**
- Small investment: **Less than $50,000**
- Low to medium complexity
- Incremental improvement to existing system or process
- Contained within a single domain or product area
- Fast value realization (typically <6 months)

**Examples:**
- Dashboard enhancement adding new data field
- Workflow automation reducing manual steps
- Report creation for operational visibility
- Minor UI/UX improvement based on user feedback
- Small integration between 2 existing systems

âœ… **Value Articulated:** Demonstrates positive value in at least ONE category:

- Customer (experience, satisfaction)
- Regulatory/Compliance (meets requirements, reduces risk)

âœ… **Budget Available:** Funding exists in product team's budget

âœ… **Scope Clear:** Request is clear and understandable

âœ… **Tier Appropriate:** Truly <$50k and low/medium complexity

âœ… **Architectural Fit:** Aligns with architectural standards (if DAR review required)

## When Does DAR Review Get Triggered?

Your Enhancement may require Domain Architecture Reviewer (DAR) review if it involves:
- Integration with other systems (even simple ones)
- Customer data or PII
- Cloud services or external APIs
- Security considerations
- Deviation from known architectural patterns

**Don't worry - your Product Team Lead will identify this and route appropriately.**

## After Approval - What to Expect

1. **Added to Product Team Backlog** - Your Enhancement joins the prioritized work queue
2. **Sprint Planning** - Product Team Lead schedules based on capacity and priority
3. **Development** - Your Enhancement is built and tested
4. **Deployment** - Solution is deployed to production
5. **Value Tracking** - Sample-based tracking (10-15% of Enhancements quarterly)

**Typical Timeline from Approval to Delivery:** 2-12 weeks depending on team capacity and priority
