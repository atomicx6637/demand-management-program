# Tier 1 Program Brief Template

**Investment Range:** >$2,000,000 (typically $5M+)
**Complexity:** High - Transformational, enterprise-wide impact
**Target Document Length:** 10 pages (plus appendices)
**Support:** Complete with Strategic Planning Office assistance

---

## Instructions for Completing This Template

**Purpose:** The Program Brief provides executive leadership with a comprehensive overview of a transformational investment opportunity. This document initiates the Tier 1 qualification process and serves as the foundation for developing the full business case.

**Who Should Complete This:**
- Program Sponsor (Executive-level)
- Business Owner
- Strategic Planning Office (support)
- Enterprise Architecture (consultation)

**Submission Process:**
1. Download this template
2. Schedule working session with Strategic Planning Office
3. Complete all sections with data, analysis, and supporting evidence
4. Review with executive sponsor for alignment
5. Submit completed brief via email to: tier1-intake@company.com
6. EA/Strategy Office will create demand record and initiate Gate 0 review

**Timeline to Complete:** 2-4 weeks with Strategic Planning support
