## What Happens Next?

Upon submission, the **EA team and Strategic Planning Office will**:

1. **Screen** your submission (2-3 business days)
   - Validate tier classification and strategic alignment
   - Engage with Strategy Office for preliminary assessment
   - Confirm executive sponsorship
   - Identify any immediate concerns or dependencies

2. **Schedule an intake interview** (60 minutes)
   - Collaborative deep-dive session with EA facilitator
   - You, executive sponsor, key stakeholders invited
   - Session will be recorded for documentation
   - Strategic context and architectural implications explored

3. **Develop comprehensive demand documentation**
   - EA team develops formal business case outline
   - Financial modeling initiated with Finance
   - Architectural assessment and integration analysis
   - No additional work required from you during initial qualification

4. **Route to governance review**
   - Complete business case developed
   - Architecture Board review scheduled
   - Tech Subcommittee review and recommendation
   - TAC presentation and funding decision

**Target Cycle Time:** 14-21 days from submission to TAC decision
