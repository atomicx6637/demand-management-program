What Happens Next - Tier 1 Gate Process

Upon submission, this intake triggers the following:

Immediate (1-2 days):
  1. Gate 0 - Initial Screening: Strategic Planning Office reviews intake
  2. Sponsor Contact: Strategy Office schedules meeting with executive sponsor
  3. Templates Provided: You receive Program Brief template and financial model
  4. Support Assigned: Strategy Office resource assigned to support development
Program Brief Development (2-4 weeks):
  1. Working sessions with Strategy Office to complete 10-page Program Brief
  2. Financial modeling with Finance team
  3. Preliminary architecture assessment with EA
  4. Stakeholder analysis and engagement planning
Gate 1 - Qualification (4-8 weeks):
  1. Comprehensive business case development
  2. Future state architecture design
  3. Detailed financial modeling and risk analysis
Governance Review:
  1. Gate 2: Architecture Board Review (7-10 days)
  2. Gate 3: Tech Subcommittee Deep Dive (2-3 hour session)
  3. Gate 4: Executive Leadership Team Pre-Briefing
  4. Gate 5: TAC Review and Funding Authorization
  5. Gate 6: Board Review (if >$10M or strategic significance)

Total Cycle Time: 6-12 weeks from intake to authorization
