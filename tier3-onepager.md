**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Audience:** Business Sponsors, Project Managers
**Questions?** Contact EA Team - [ea-team@quantumenergy.com](mailto:ea-team@quantumenergy.com)

## What Qualifies as Tier 3?

âœ… **Your project should be submitted as Tier 3 if it meets:**

**Cost + Complexity Matrix:**
- **$50k-$2M + Low Complexity**, OR
- **$50k-$500k + Medium Complexity**

**Project Characteristics:**
- Discrete project with defined scope and deliverables
- May involve multiple systems or business processes
- Some architectural considerations
- Value realization within 12-18 months

**Examples:**
- GPS tracking system implementation for fleet management
- Middleware platform upgrade
- Proof-of-concept for new technology
- Single-system replacement or modernization
- Operational technology system enhancement

## Complexity Assessment Guide

**Low Complexity:**
- Single system or contained process
- Minimal integration (1-2 systems)
- Proven technology
- Single department impact

**Medium Complexity:**
- Multiple systems (2-4 integrations)
- Cross-functional impact
- Mix of proven and new technology
- Moderate process redesign

**High Complexity** â†’ Should be Tier 2:
- Enterprise-wide impact
- Extensive integration (5+ systems)
- New technology introduction
- Transformational change

## How to Submit - **Interview-Based Process**

### **Step 1: Submit High-Level Intake (5-10 minutes)**

Go to ServiceNow â†’ **Submit Demand Request** â†’ Select **"Tier 3 Project"**

**You'll complete a simple 1-page form:**
- Project title and contact information
- Strategic objective alignment
- Estimated cost and complexity (Low/Medium)
- High-level business need (3-5 sentences)
- Who will benefit?
- High-level solution overview (2-3 sentences)
- Systems/platforms likely involved
- Expected value categories (check boxes with brief descriptions)
- Desired timeline

**That's it! No lengthy business case required at this stage.**

---

### **Step 2: EA Team Schedules Interview**

**Within 1-2 business days**, EA team will:
- Screen your submission
- Schedule a **30-45 minute interview** with you (and key stakeholders)
- Send you interview prep guide

---

### **Step 3: Participate in Interview (30-45 minutes)**

**EA facilitator will ask about:**
- Business need deep dive
- Solution approach and alternatives
- Value across all 5 categories
- Complexity and feasibility
- Risks and constraints

**The interview is recorded** (Teams/Zoom with transcription) so EA can develop comprehensive documentation.

---

### **Step 4: EA Develops Your Business Case**

**You don't write the business case - EA does!**

EA team will:
- Review interview recording
- Draft 5-7 page Tier 3 Business Case using template
- Add architectural analysis
- Develop rough financial model (with Finance support if needed)
- Share draft with you for validation

**Your role:** Review draft and confirm accuracy (15-minute call or async)

---

### **Step 5: Governance Review**

**Technology Subcommittee** (bi-weekly) reviews your business case and recommends to TAC
**TAC** (monthly) makes funding decision

**You'll be invited to present** (typically 10-15 minutes)

## EA Intake Interview - What to Expect

**Format:** 30-45 minute recorded Teams/Zoom call

**Who Attends:**
- You (Business Sponsor)
- Key SMEs (if needed)
- EA facilitator (or DAR for domain-specific projects)
- Finance (optional, if complex financial modeling)

**Agenda:**
1. Introduction (5 min) - Process overview
2. Business Need (10 min) - What problem? For whom? What if we don't?
3. Solution Approach (10 min) - Proposed solution, alternatives, deliverables
4. Value Exploration (10 min) - Value across 5 categories, measurement
5. Complexity & Feasibility (5 min) - Architecture, integration, resources, risks
6. Next Steps (5 min) - Timeline, governance process

**Interview Prep Guide** will be sent with specific questions to consider.
